our mission

After years of watching friends and family deal with unexplained health problems, I started paying closer attention to what we were actually eating. What I found was hard to ignore — microplastics and synthetic chemicals were showing up everywhere, even in things we thought were simple or safe.

Sourdough is one of the oldest, most nourishing foods we have. But even that’s been affected — starters stored in plastic tubs, stirred with plastic spoons, fermented in synthetic containers. It didn’t sit right.

Flour Department started with one belief: the food that keeps us going should be clean, real, and as close to nature as possible.

We're here to bring truly plastic-free, wild sourdough culture back into kitchens — for health, for taste, and because the process matters. It's not about going backwards. It’s about doing things with care again.
